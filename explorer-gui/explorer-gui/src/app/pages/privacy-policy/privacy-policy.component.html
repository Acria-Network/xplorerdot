4. Cookies

If you review or download information, we may track the visit through the use of “cookies”. Cookies are pieces of information that a web site transfers to an individual’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes. The use of cookies is an industry standard, and you will find cookies at most major web sites. Cookies help WEB3SCAN to tailor information to your preferences and to see which areas of the web site are frequented most. WEB3SCAN never uses cookies to retrieve information from your computer that was not originally sent in a cookie. Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies. If you prefer, you can change your browser settings to refuse cookies. However, in so doing, you may not be able to fully utilize the Portal.
